Hack the Evening Away

An inclusive and social hackathon like no other.

Saturday, June 30
5pm - 12:30am

Sign up today

What is Hack the Evening Away? A new breed of hackathon.

Hack the Evening Away seeks to bring inclusivity to the often inaccessible world of hackathons. Join us for food, drink, conversation and coding this Saturday at 5pm in Bryn Mawr College's Bettws-y-Coed building. Questions are encouraged, mentors will be present, and the overarching gole of the night is to create.
